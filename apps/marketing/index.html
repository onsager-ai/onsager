Who this is for

You are a senior or staff engineer who's already letting AI agents write code. The diff is fine; the policy around the diff is the problem. Onsager is the policy layer.

Not for: vibe-coders shipping unreviewed AI output, hobbyists, or large-enterprise procurement-led buyers.

What you do

  1. ① Describe the policy in plain English ("auto-merge PRs labeled auto-merge once CI is green and a Verify gate passes").
  2. ② Onsager proposes a workflow draft you review and edit.
  3. ③ Bind it to a repo when you're ready. Your draft is yours before, during, and after.
